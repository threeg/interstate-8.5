Ocean Breathes Salty

"I wrote it in about ten minutes, on a ferry, looking at the water." — Isaac Brock
LYRICS
The ocean breathes salty, well, today
I could hardly hear the words you were saying
I hear ya' but barely, this is my only chance
I looked at you, then looked away
Kept lookin' out when the day turns gray
And your smile was the only thing
That should've made my day, but it just made me sadder still
NOTES
Written for a friend who died in a car accident; the b-side "Whenever You Breathe Out, I Breathe In" is its companion piece.
